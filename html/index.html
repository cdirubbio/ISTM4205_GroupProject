What is ActivityFinder?

ActivityFinder is an application that allows users to search for activities in their vacation destination. Have you ever found yourself bored on vacation, looking for something to do? Us too! Which is why we created a platform that will eliminate this problem.

What will ActivityFinder do for me?

Our proprietary web application provides users like yourself with the ability find stuff to do while on vacation. Whether you're in Prague, the Bahamas or even just a beach near you, we have activities for vacation spots all over the world.

Some of ActivityFinder's Features Includes:

  • Keyword Search
  • Location Filtering
  • Favorites
  • FAQ and Support Form

About Us

ActivityFinder emerged from George Washington University's Web Application Development course, a fertile ground for innovation within the ISTM program. The startup is the brainchild of our dedicated founders: CEO Christian DiRubbio, CTO Nick Pelekanos, CIO Anthony Minder, and CFO Sean Vaghedi. Christian's passion for travel and technology served as the catalyst for ActivityFinder's inception. Nick's technical expertise and problem-solving prowess have been crucial in shaping the platform's capabilities. Together, this visionary team is poised to revolutionize how travelers uncover and engage in activities during their journeys, promising a seamless and enriching travel experience for adventurers worldwide.
