What is ActivityFinder?

ActivityFinder is an application that allows users to search for activities in their vacation destination. Have you ever found yourself bored on vacation, looking for something to do? Us too! Which is why we created a platform that will eliminate this problem.

What will ActivityFinder do for me?

Our proprietary web application provides users like yourself with the ability find stuff to do while on vacation. Whether you're in Prague, the Bahamas or even just a beach near you, we have activities for vacation spots all over the world.

How do I search for activities?

It is very simple actually. Just go into the search page and type in what activity you are interested.

Why is the search page not showing any activities?

The system actually works best when you use few keywords so please try typing less words.

How often do you update your activities?

We have a wide selection of activites provided for each location and we continue rolling out more and more daily.

What does it cost to register?

Monthly subscription starts at $19.99 and we offer a Yearly Subscription plan for just $99.99

How do I know if I will like it?

We offer a 3-day trial where you can explore the capabilities of ActivityFinder and see if you like it.

Does ActivityFinder work outside the United States?

Unfortunately not at the moment because we air aiming to maximize our quality domestically, but we plan to go international eventually.

Are there any system requirements for using the product/service?

No ActivityFinder runs on Safari, Chrome, Firefox, Internet Explorer, Microsoft Edge and Opera.
